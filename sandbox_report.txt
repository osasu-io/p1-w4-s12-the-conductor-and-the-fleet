QUESTION 2: Why must a SOC analyst NEVER use "Bridged" mode when detonating unknown malware?
ANSWER: Bridged mode connects the VM directly to the real network, giving malware access to all other 
devices and the internet. This allows it to spread throughout the network to other devices.
